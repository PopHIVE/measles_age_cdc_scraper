Measles cases in 2025

As of February 27, 2025, a total of 164 measles cases were reported by 9 jurisdictions: Alaska, California, Georgia, Kentucky, New Jersey, New Mexico, New York City, Rhode Island, and Texas. Find more real-time updates of measles cases reported by:

Texas Department of State Health Services
New Mexico Department of Health

There have been 3 outbreaks (defined as 3 or more related cases) reported in 2025, and 93% of cases (153 of 164) are outbreak-associated. For comparison, 16 outbreaks were reported during 2024 and 69% of cases (198 of 285) were outbreak-associated.
